A collaboration between performers Tia Ashley Kushniruk 亚 女弟 and Susannah Haight with live lighting and set by Darren Shaen, **GAPE** warps and reimagines the religious image of the divine female figure as a monstrous, bleeding and liberated creature with blood red acrylic nails. As the two female figures parade, pray, exercise, scream, hide and disembowel each other, the audience is asked to redefine what makes a woman sacred - her body or her actions?
